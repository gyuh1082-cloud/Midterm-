Inclusive Harlem

Redesigning Montefiore Square for comfort, dignity, and shared public use.

Problem

The plaza does not equally support all users. This redesign focuses on accessibility, comfort, and inclusion for diverse Harlem communities.

Site Understanding

Inclusive green space with seating, trees, and open lawn areas supporting rest and comfort.

Active plaza with circulation, seating, and diverse users creating a shared environment.

Final Proposal

A redesigned plaza that supports accessibility, dignity, and shared public life in Harlem.
